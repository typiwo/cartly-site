# Clem

**AI-powered grocery health scoring for iOS.**

Clem helps you make smarter grocery choices. Scan a product and get a 0-100 health score, an AI-generated analysis of ingredients and additives, personalized dietary alerts, and healthier alternatives — all in seconds.

Coming to the App Store soon.
